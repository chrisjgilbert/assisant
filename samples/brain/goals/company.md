Northwind is a B2B product-analytics SaaS. Company-level priorities this quarter:

1. **Land usage-based billing (UBB).** Move from seat-based to usage-based pricing so revenue tracks value delivered. The Acme pilot is the proof point — it must meter real usage by end of Q2.
2. **Earn billing trust.** Billing errors are existential for a paid product; zero tolerance for customer-visible billing/invoicing defects.
3. **Keep enterprise pilots happy.** Acme is the lighthouse account for UBB and the reference for future deals.
